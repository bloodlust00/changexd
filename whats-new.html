Next Generation Capabilities

AI Changeover Prediction

Machine learning algorithms will analyze historical production data to predict changeover time, detect bottlenecks, and suggest optimal machine setup before production starts.

Real-Time Factory Command Center

Managers will monitor all factories through a centralized digital dashboard displaying line efficiency, machine utilization, and changeover progress in real time.

IoT Connected Sewing Machines

Industrial machines will automatically send production data, idle time, and machine status directly to the platform, eliminating manual tracking.

Digital Twin Simulation

Factories will simulate production lines virtually to test line balancing and production schedules before executing them on the shop floor.

Digital Transformation Roadmap

Phase 1 – Digital SMED Monitoring

Track changeover activities, line status and SAM comparison digitally.

Phase 2 – Smart Factory Dashboard

Real-time monitoring of machine utilization, operator efficiency and line productivity.

Phase 3 – AI Production Intelligence

Predict delays, recommend line balancing strategies and optimize production flow.

Phase 4 – Fully Autonomous Factory

Machines, operators and managers connected through a digital ecosystem.
